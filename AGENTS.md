# AGENTS.md

## Mission
Build a production-quality standalone SEO/PWA article page for a legal-sensitive/YMYL topic.

## Non-negotiables
- One H1 only.
- Mobile-first.
- Performance and Accessibility target: Lighthouse 90+.
- No legal advice. Educational content only.
- Attorney-controlled workflow: legal team controls legal strategy; documentation team supports research, sources, citations, and draft materials for attorney review.
- No invented sources, laws, cases, or statistics.
- Structured data must match visible content.
- No VideoObject while the video is only a placeholder.
- No analytics/marketing cookies by default.
- Email popup must include privacy microcopy and must not request sensitive personal identifiers.
