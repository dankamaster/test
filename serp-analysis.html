High-Frequency Domains

The dominant domains showed that this SERP is not ordinary blog SEO. It combines institutional sources, legal-aid resources, PDFs, law-library guides, and commercial expert/service pages.

  • euaa.europa.eu — COI methodology / EU authority.
  • immigrationequality.org — US asylum manual and practical country conditions resources.
  • tahirih.org — expert witness and affidavit/sample packet PDFs.
  • cgrs.uclawsf.edu — CGRS manuals and expert witness database.
  • immigrationjustice.us — practical asylum training and sample packet resources.
  • unhcr.org / refworld.org — international source layer.
  • countryconditions.expert and similar commercial pages — service intent layer.
