# Conversion and Privacy Plan

## Primary conversion
Email-first documentation request.

## Popup
Headline: Request a COI Documentation Scope by Email

Fields:
- Work email
- Country or region
- Document needed
- Deadline
- Message

Privacy microcopy:
We use your email to respond to this documentation request. Please do not include sensitive personal identifiers or confidential client details unless your legal team has approved this communication method.

Marketing checkbox:
Optional, unchecked by default.

## Cookie plan
No analytics or advertising cookies are loaded by default. If analytics are added later, load only after consent and provide accept/reject/manage options.
