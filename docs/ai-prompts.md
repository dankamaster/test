# AI Prompts

## Prompt 1 — SEO research
Analyze the SERP and keyword data for a legal-sensitive topic: "How to Prepare a Country of Origin Information Report for a US Asylum Case". Separate informational, attorney-facing, and commercial expert-service intent. Produce primary/secondary keywords, competitor gaps, ICP, CTA, and H2/H3 outline.

## Prompt 2 — YMYL article
Draft a 1,200–1,800 word English article for immigration attorneys and legal teams. It must be educational only, not legal advice. Separate legal strategy from documentation support. Include expert commentary blocks, email CTA blocks, source hierarchy, checklist, FAQ, sources, and AI/manual note.

## Prompt 3 — Static page implementation
Build a standalone mobile-first HTML/CSS/JS page with one H1, semantic landmarks, FAQ, author block, disclaimer, email popup with privacy microcopy, PWA manifest, service worker, Open Graph/Twitter tags, and JSON-LD Article/WebPage/Organization/Person/Breadcrumb/FAQ schema.
